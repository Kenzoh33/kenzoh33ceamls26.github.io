  Today was a very long day filled with research and running various experiments on our cross-modal CFD. I was able to find out that the DCA of our fusion model was greater than all four baseline classification models given by our manual. This proved our model was accurate and the domain theory we worked on was actually proving right. I further passed our fusion model through the respiratory tiebreaker signal, which was stored to use in case of disagreements between ECG and PPG classification. We were able to resolve such disagreements through the RESP signal introduced as the LEVEL 3 validation checkpoint on our trust hierarchy.
